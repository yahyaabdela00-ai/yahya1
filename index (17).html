The Person

Born in Ethiopia.
Building Africa's AI future.

Yahya Mohammed Abdela is a founder and AI researcher born in Dire Dawa, Ethiopia — driven by a single conviction: Africa should be building AI, not just consuming it.

With over seven years in the tech industry, he launched a global tech and AI research lab in East Africa in 2026 — bringing together international developers from across the world to work on the continent's most ambitious projects.

The mission is simple. Build real solutions for real problems, at scale, with the best people — and take it global by 2028.
